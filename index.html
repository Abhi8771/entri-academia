Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA,It is an undergraduate degree course designed to equip students with the fundamental knowledge and skills required to excel in various fields of business management and administration.

Master of Business Administration

MBA provides theoretical and practical training for business or investment management graduates. An MBA is designed to help graduates gain a better understanding of business management functions.

Bachelor of Computer Applications

BCA,It is an undergraduate program spanning around three years that equips students with the latest trends and technologies of the ever-evolving Information Technology (IT) industry.

Bachelor of Visual Communication

BVC is a 3-year undergraduate degree course. It offers training in essential visual communication skills that will enable the student to make a career in visual communication in creative fields such as photography, web designing, and advertising.

Master of Commerce

MCom is a postgraduate commerce-oriented course that can be pursued by the student after the undergraduate commerce course,helps to establish a career in any of the following fields; commerce, accounting, economics, and management.

Bachelor of Fashion Technology

Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.F. Tech) is a four-year undergraduate program which provides knowledge, techniques and methods required in the production of fashion accessories and fashion garments.
